流浪地球计划(行动计划)

第一阶段【刹车时代】：用50年的时间研发制造行星发动机，使发动机喷口固定在地球运行的反方向；

THE FIRST STAGE [BRAKE ERA] : WITH 50 YEARS OF TIME TO DEVELOP AND MANUFACTURE PLANETARY ENGINES, SO THAT THE ENGINE NOZZLE IS FIXED IN THE OPPOSITE DIRECTION OF THE EARTH’S OPERATION;

第二阶段【逃逸时代】：全功率开动行星发动机，使地球加速到逃逸速度，飞出太阳系。

THE SECOND STAGE [ESCAPE ERA] : THE FULL POWER OF THE PLANETARY ENGINE TO ACCELERATE THE EARTH TO ESCAPE VELOCITY, FLY OUT OF THE SOLAR SYSTEM.

第三阶段【流浪时代Ⅰ(加速) 】：地球在外太空继续加速，飞向比邻星；这个阶段，地球脱离太阳系轨道，飞向目标，不间断地加速500年，加速至光速的千分之五，然后地球以光速的千分之五滑行1300年。

THE THIRD STAGE [WANDERING AGE I (ACCELERATION)] : THE EARTH CONTINUES TO ACCELERATE IN OUTER SPACE, FLYING TO PROXIMA CENTAURI; IN THIS PHASE, THE EARTH LEAVES THE ORBIT OF THE SOLAR SYSTEM, FLIES TOWARDS THE TARGET, ACCELERATES CONTINUOUSLY FOR 500 YEARS, ACCELERATES TO 5 THOUSANDTHS OF THE SPEED OF LIGHT, AND THEN THE EARTH GLIDES AT 5 THOUSANDTHS OF THE SPEED OF LIGHT FOR 1,300 YEARS.

第四阶段【流浪时代Ⅱ(减速) 】：地球掉转方向，用行星发动机开始长达500多年的减速；再用100多年的时间，泊入比邻星的轨道。

THE FOURTH STAGE (WANDERING AGE II (DECELERATION)) : THE EARTH TURNS THE DIRECTION, AND THE PLANETARY ENGINE BEGINS TO DECELERATE FOR MORE THAN 500 YEARS; IT WILL TAKE MORE THAN 100 YEARS TO STAY IN ORBIT AROUND PROXIMA CENTAURI.

第五阶段【新太阳时代】：地球成为目标恒星系新的行星，人类抵达新家园。

THE FIFTH STAGE [NEW SOLAR AGE] : EARTH BECOMES A NEW PLANET IN THE TARGET STAR SYSTEM, AND HUMANITY ARRIVES AT A NEW HOME.
